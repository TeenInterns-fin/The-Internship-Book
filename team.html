Meet the Team

The Teen Interns Book is a collaborative project created entirely by talented teenage interns. Get to know the amazing individuals who worked together to bring this book to life.

M

Myrah Sinha

Writer

Myrah Sinha is one of the lead writers behind The Teen Internship Book. As a high school student with a deep passion for storytelling, law, and social impact, Myrah has always believed in making complex ideas feel accessible. Whether it's conducting interviews, drafting thoughtful guides, or building a bridge between teens and the professional world, Myrah’s voice is honest, sharp, and distinctly Gen Z. Outside of writing, you’ll find her debating policy, leading initiatives, or dreaming up the next big idea that could change the world.
