Written by Teens, For Teens

This book breaks the mold of traditional career guides. Created by 23 teenagers who've actually been through the internship journey, it's packed with real stories, practical strategies, and the kind of honest advice you won't find anywhere else.

No Adult BS: Just straight talk from people who remember what it's like to be completely lost about internships.
Actually Useful: Every tip, template, and strategy has been tested by real teens in real situations.

Your Digital Companion

Step into the future of learning with our multimedia-rich experience. Every QR code is a gateway to exclusive content created by teens, for teens—bringing the internship world to life beyond the printed page.

Video Content: Watch raw, unfiltered moments from our teen contributors as they share their biggest internship wins, epic fails, and everything in between.The whole book is visual and filled with illustrations.
Mixtape, Podcasts and Vlogs: Listen & watch honest conversations about imposter syndrome, networking anxiety, and how to bounce back when things don't go as planned. It is a complete visual and auditory delight.

Eco-Friendly

Printed once, updated forever. Our eco-conscious approach means fresh content in real time without the environmental cost.

Own one copy: Recycle. One copy is all you need to stay updated in almost real time. A phygital ( Physical and digital book ).
Stay relevant: This is one gift that updates itself. From Legacy to new age.

Impactful

Every book sold helps sponsor access for those who can't afford it — because everyone deserves a chance to grow.

Accelerate: Not everyone has the same starting line to their dreams. Accelerate the one behind. Boost them by giving their dreams a push.
Invest to support: Every copy sold or gifted to a loved one contributes to Mentorship and internships sponsored for students who can't afford it.

About the Book

World's #1 Student Internship Book

The Internship Book - What, Why and How is the world's number one multimedia guide on student internships, for teens by teens! It's part handbook, part hype squad, and all things internship! From finding the right gig to surviving your first day, this book spills the tea on it all. Created by teenagers who've been there, done that (and drawn it too), it's your no-stress go-to guide to stepping into the professional world.

Internships just got a glow-up, and this book is leading the way!

What is an internship?

Understanding the basics and exploring different types of opportunities available to teenagers. From virtual internships to hands-on experiences, we cover it all.

  • Types of internships available
  • Virtual vs. in-person opportunities
  • Industry-specific internships
  • Duration and commitment levels

Why do teens need internships?

Exploring the transformative benefits and long-term importance for your future career development and personal growth.

  • Real-world experience
  • Professional networking
  • Skill development
  • Career clarity and direction

How to get internships?

Step-by-step strategies, proven techniques, and insider tips to find, apply for, and succeed in your dream internship.

  • Application strategies
  • Resume and cover letter tips
  • Interview preparation
  • Follow-up best practices
